Well, as a matter of relief, you are not the only one to feel that way. All of us, at some point in their academic life have unmistakably been in this spot.


What Casues Exam Fear?

We must understand the causes of fear or stress during the exams. There are no solutions without the problems. It is important to find out causes affecting students� minds during exams. Students preparing for exams are always under pressure. The pressure develops into anxiety and nervousness which can affect your daily life. It can lead to poor performance in exams as well. Addressing these causes can help in understanding the emotions and regulate anxiety level.



CASUES OF EXAM FEAR

  1. Emotional Pressure

  2. External Pressure

  3. Internal Pressure

  4. Lack of Preparation

  5. LifeStyle

  6. Poor Studying Habits

How to deal with Examination Fear?


Sometimes dealing with stress can be difficult but however it can be prevented. To deal with the stress, systematic, organized and planned effort from the beginning of the session should be implemented. Destressing methods can be adopted by students if they feel stressed due to exams. Here are a few tips that can help you deal with stress during exams.


Physical Activities


Students can practice breathing techniques by meditation. It will increase the concentration of the student during the exam. Relaxing your eyes is very important as it provides long term concentration. Regular exercise will help to reduce the stress. Exercises such as cycling, walking, jogging, playing sports etc can be useful to overcome stress.


Cleaning Study Area



Reduce cluttering around your study table. Keep your study area neat and clean. Arrange the necessary materials required for the exam. Have you heard �cluttered desk is a sign of a cluttered mind�? That is the reason where parents keep reminding us to keep the room clean. A clean place can help you think and understand better.


Setting Goals


You must set realistic goals before the exams. That will help you to put everything in perspective. You have to accept the situation and strength on how much you can maximize your productivity without causing stress for yourself.


Sleep


Sleeping habits should be maintained. Early to bed and early to rise can help students to concentrate in preparing for exams. Proper sleep should be given to the body for good retention power. Studying full night is not the right way to prepare for an exam as it can spoil the health


Mental Stability


Students get distracted easily. They should focus more on preparing for exams than indulging in other activities like playing games, watching tv, etc. A mind that thinks positively can cope up with any situation.


Lifestyle


The most important thing to overcome stress is by changing your lifestyle for the better. Students should develop a routine for a positive and healthy mind. Maintaining a healthy diet with nutritious food is very important. They should inculcate the habit of morning walk for refreshing mind and soul. Getting enough sleep at night keeps you physically and mentally fit for the rest of the day. Listening to music can be an added advantage for relaxing your mind.


Reduce Phone Usage


Have you ever thought that mobile phones can cause stress? Well, research has shown that overuse of mobile phones will not only cause stress but also will affect mental health. Try to avoid using the phone during study time. Keep it silent or use it when you�re done with the studies.


Prioritize Time


Before the exam starts, try to figure out how early you need to start revising. Make the study plan according to that and break it down if you want to study day by day. Never overestimate and take how much time you can allocate for each day. Make sure you invest more time in difficult topics in the initial stage of preparation followed by simpler ones. Managing your study plan can help relieve stress.
